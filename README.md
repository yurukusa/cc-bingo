# CC Bingo

**How many of these have happened to you?**

Claude Code bingo card generator. 50 relatable moments, randomized into a 5x5 grid. Click to mark, get bingo, share the pain.

**[Get Your Card](https://yurukusa.github.io/cc-bingo/)**

## How It Works

1. Get a randomly generated bingo card
2. Click/tap squares that have happened to you
3. Get bingo (row, column, or diagonal)
4. Share your result

## Sample Squares

- "AI fixes a bug, introduces two new ones"
- "You say 'be concise', AI writes 500 lines"
- "/compact triggers at the worst moment"
- "AI apologizes for something it didn't do"
- "Pre-commit hook saves the day"
- "You approve a tool call without reading it"
